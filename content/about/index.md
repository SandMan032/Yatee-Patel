Hi, I'm Yatee — a CS student building systems, with a focus on backend infrastructure and automation.
{{< /lead >}}

This blog is my open engineering notebook. I use it to think in public — working out designs by writing them down, and keeping a record of what I'm learning as I build.

## What I'm building

The common thread across what I build is systems that have to be correct under pressure — low-latency data pipelines, strict validation gates, resilient routing between services, and logic that stays honest when things go wrong. Right now that's showing up in backend and infra work; expect the details to shift as I do.

- **[Learning Log](/learning-log/)** — notes on the tools and ideas I'm picking up.
