{{< lead >}}
Hi, I'm Yatee — a CS student building a live algorithmic trading bot for Indian
equity derivatives.
{{< /lead >}}

This blog is my open engineering notebook. I use it to think in public: to work
out designs by writing them down, and to keep a record of what I learn about Go,
Docker and systems design along the way.

## What I'm building

A live trading bot — the interesting part isn't the strategy, it's everything
around it: low-latency data ingestion, a strict pre-trade risk gate, resilient
order routing, and reconciliation that stays honest when things go wrong.

## What I write about

- **[Bot Architecture](/bot-architecture/)** — deep dives on the system's design.
- **[Learning Log](/learning-log/)** — notes on the tools and ideas I'm picking up.
- **[Misc](/misc/)** — shorter, essay-style posts.
